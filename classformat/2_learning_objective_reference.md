Learning Objective
 
Authentication, authorization, and access control are fundamental concepts in ensuring the security of systems and resources. Authentication verifies the identity of users or entities, ensuring they are who they claim to be. Authorization determines the actions or operations that authenticated users can perform, based on their privileges and permissions. Access control regulates and enforces the restrictions on resource access, safeguarding against unauthorized entry or misuse.
Focus: Authentication, Authorization, Access Control, Security Principles and Best Practices, Access Control Technologies, Regulatory Compliance and Standards
Duration: 2 Hours
Pre-requisite: Basic Knowledge of Security
