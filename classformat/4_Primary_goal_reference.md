Primary Goal 
Verify the identity of users or entities attempting to access a system or resource. Prevent unauthorized access and protect against impersonation or identity theft.
Ensure that only authorized individuals can gain access to sensitive information or perform specific actions.
Determine and enforce the actions or operations that authenticated users or entities are allowed to perform within a system.
Grant appropriate permissions and privileges based on roles, responsibilities, or attributes. Prevent unauthorized or malicious activities by restricting access to sensitive functions or data.
Regulate and control access to resources based on predefined policies and rules. Ensure that users or entities can only access the resources they are authorized to use.
Protect sensitive information from unauthorized disclosure, modification, or deletion.
